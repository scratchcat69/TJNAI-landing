DipCue Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

👋 Overview

DipCue ("we", "us") is built by TJNAI LLC. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

📋 What We Collect

DipCue collects the following information:

Email address — for account creation and authentication.

Pool configuration — name, volume, and type you enter in the app.

Pool chemistry readings — scan results stored in your history.

🚫 What We Do Not Collect

  • Your name
  • Location data
  • Any other personal information beyond what is listed above

🎯 How We Use It

Your pool data is used exclusively to provide the app's features — calculating chemical recommendations and storing your reading history.

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

📷 Camera

DipCue requests camera access solely to photograph your test strip. Strip images are sent to our AI analysis service and deleted immediately after analysis completes. Images are never stored long-term.

🗄️ Data Storage

Your data is stored securely by Supabase, a US-based cloud infrastructure provider. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

🤝 Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your pool chemistry data is private to your account.

💳 In-App Purchases

DipCue offers a one-time lifetime purchase processed through Apple's App Store. Purchase history is managed by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not store payment information. RevenueCat may collect anonymized purchase and device identifiers to verify entitlements.

🔔 Push Notifications

If you enable notifications, DipCue uses your device's push notification token to send optional weekly pool-check reminders. You can disable notifications at any time in your device Settings.

🗑️ Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at support@tjnai.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

👶 Children

DipCue is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

🔔 Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you within the app. Continued use after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

✉️ Contact

Questions? Contact us at support@tjnai.com.
