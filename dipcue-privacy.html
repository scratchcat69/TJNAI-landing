DipCue Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 6, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 6, 2026

👋 Who We Are

DipCue is operated by TJNAI.ai LLC, a North Carolina limited liability company. TJNAI.ai LLC is the data controller for all personal data collected through DipCue.

Email: support@tjnai.ai
Website: tjnai.ai

📋 What We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the app:

DataPurpose
Email addressAccount creation and authentication
Pool configuration (name, volume, type, strip preference)Generating accurate chemical recommendations
Pool chemistry readings (chlorine, pH, alkalinity, CYA, hardness)Storing your test history
Device push tokenWeekly pool reminders (optional, requires your permission)
Anonymized purchase and device identifiersVerifying your in-app purchase entitlement
Security audit logsDetecting and preventing abuse

We do not collect your name, location, or any information unrelated to pool management.

Camera & photos: The app requests camera access to photograph test strips. Images are transmitted to our AI analysis service and permanently deleted immediately after analysis completes. No strip photos are stored anywhere.

What is required vs. optional: Your email address and pool configuration are required to use DipCue — without them the app cannot function. Pool chemistry readings are required to generate recommendations. Your push notification token is entirely optional; you can use DipCue fully without enabling notifications.

⚖️ Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and United Kingdom, we process your data under the following legal bases:

DataLegal Basis
Account and pool dataContract — necessary to provide the service you signed up for
Push notification tokenConsent — only collected when you enable reminders; you can withdraw at any time in Settings
Purchase identifiersLegitimate interest — necessary to verify your purchase entitlement
Security audit logsLegitimate interest — protecting the integrity and security of our service

🤖 Automated Decision-Making

DipCue uses AI to analyze your test strip photo and calculate chemical dose recommendations. This analysis is a tool to assist you — it does not constitute a legally binding or similarly significant automated decision under GDPR Article 22.

You remain in full control: all recommendations are advisory, and no action is taken on your pool or account without your explicit input. You can dispute or ignore any recommendation at any time.

🎯 How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Provide pool chemistry analysis and recommendations
  • Store your reading history
  • Send weekly pool testing reminders (only if you opt in)
  • Verify your in-app purchase
  • Detect and prevent abuse or unauthorized access

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to pool management. We do not sell your data to any third party.

🗄️ Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account — either through the app's Profile → Delete Account button or by contacting us — all your data (profile, readings, push token, and audit logs) is permanently and immediately deleted from our systems.

We do not retain any data after account deletion.

🛡️ Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

All users
  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Update inaccurate data (most can be updated directly in the app)
  • Deletion — Delete your account and all data instantly via Profile → Delete Account, or by emailing support@tjnai.ai
  • Portability — Request your data in a machine-readable format (CSV or JSON)
EEA and UK users (GDPR)
  • Restriction — Ask us to pause processing of your data without deleting it
  • Object — Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent — Disable push notifications at any time in iOS Settings or in the app
  • Lodge a complaint — Contact your local Data Protection Authority. In the EU: edpb.europa.eu. In the UK: ico.org.uk.
California users (CCPA)
  • You have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion, and to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@tjnai.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

👶 Children

DipCue is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of digital consent. In the United States this is age 13 (COPPA). Under GDPR, the threshold is age 16, though EU member states may lower this to 13. We apply the higher standard where applicable.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at support@tjnai.ai and we will delete it promptly.

🇪🇺 EU & UK Representative

TJNAI.ai LLC is a United States company. As a small business whose processing of EEA and UK personal data is limited in scale and does not involve sensitive data categories, we currently rely on the Article 27 GDPR exemption for occasional processors and have not appointed a formal EU or UK representative.

EEA and UK residents may direct all privacy enquiries and data subject requests directly to us at support@tjnai.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

🔐 Security

We protect your data with:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest
  • Row-level security on our database — your data is only accessible by you
  • Secure token storage on your device (iOS Keychain)
  • Automatic deletion of strip photos immediately after analysis
