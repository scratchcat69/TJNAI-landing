AquaIQ Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 19, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 19, 2026

👋 Overview

AquaIQ ("we," "us," "our") is developed and operated by TJNAI LLC. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

📋 What We Collect

  • Email address — collected at account creation for authentication
  • Pool configuration — name, volume, and type you enter in the app
  • Pool chemistry readings — scan results stored in your history
  • Strip images — photos taken by your camera are sent to our AI analysis service and deleted immediately after analysis completes; images are never stored long-term
  • Device token — collected for push notification reminders (only if you grant permission)
  • Purchase information — processed by RevenueCat; we do not store payment card details

🚫 What We Do Not Collect

  • Your name
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Any data beyond what is listed above

🎯 How We Use It

Your data is used exclusively to provide AquaIQ's features — analyzing test strips, calculating chemical recommendations, and storing your reading history.

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

📷 Camera

AquaIQ requests camera access solely to photograph your pool test strip. Images are transmitted to Anthropic's Claude AI API for analysis and deleted immediately after results are returned. Images are never stored on our servers.

🔗 Third-Party Services

🗄️
Supabase — supabase.com Stores your account data and pool chemistry history. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
🤖
Anthropic Claude API — anthropic.com Processes strip images for AI analysis. Images are not retained after analysis.
💳
RevenueCat — revenuecat.com Manages in-app purchases and entitlements.

🔐 Biometrics

If you enable Face ID / Touch ID, biometric authentication is handled entirely by iOS and never leaves your device. We do not access or store biometric data.

🤝 Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties beyond the service providers listed above, who are bound by their own privacy policies.

🗑️ Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at support@tjnai.ai. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

👶 Children

AquaIQ is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children. Always exercise caution when testing near water. AquaIQ is not responsible for accidents or injuries that occur during pool testing.

🔔 Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you within the app. Continued use after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
